Simple Beef Burger

Description

A tasty homemade beef burger with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Minced beef (or plant-based alternative)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion
  • Cheese (optional), ketchup, mayo
