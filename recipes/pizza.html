Classic Margherita Pizza

Description

A simple, classic Margherita pizza with tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella and basil.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or ready-made base
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil, salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Slice mozzarella and place evenly on top.
  4. Bake 10–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melted.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and a drizzle of olive oil.
